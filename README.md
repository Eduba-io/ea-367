# Xpece Drones × Eduba

A single-page landing site prepared for the Xpece founder and small team, designed to be opened on a phone at Booth NS9 during the eMerge Americas 2026 pitch. Carries the Eduba frame (60 / 30 / 10) applied to a consumer drone brand exploring a defense motion, the Feeld case study translated into dual-use vocabulary, the ICM paper, Jake's credibility, and a direct calendar CTA to Matt Creamer. Brand tokens are drawn from xpece.com (Shopify): black/green palette, Montserrat-class display face, short declarative sentence rhythm.

Intended URL path: /for/xpece-drones

Copy treats the defense pivot as stated intent, not existing product. Nothing on the page claims a defense SKU Xpece has not shipped.
